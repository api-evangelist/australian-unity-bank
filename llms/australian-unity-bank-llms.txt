# Australian Unity Bank

> Australian Unity Bank Limited is the banking arm of Australian Unity, a member-owned Australian mutual founded in Melbourne in 1840. As an APRA-authorised deposit-taking institution (ADI), it offers everyday transaction and savings accounts, term deposits, and home loans to members. It operates a Consumer Data Right (CDR / Open Banking) gateway exposing the mandated Product Reference Data (PRD) surface under the DSB Consumer Data Standards; it publishes no separate developer portal.

## APIs

- [Australian Unity Bank CDR Product Reference Data API](https://consumerdatastandardsaustralia.github.io/standards/#banking-products): Public, unauthenticated GET /cds-au/v1/banking/products surface at https://open-banking.australianunity.com.au/ (WAF/geo/accreditation-gated; returned HTTP 403 to a July 2026 non-AU probe). Contract is the shared DSB Consumer Data Standards.

## Specs
